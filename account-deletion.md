# FitIn — Account & Data Deletion

**App:** FitIn (`com.nfiny.fitin`)  
**Developer:** nfiny-jae  
**Last updated:** 12 July 2026

This page explains how to delete your FitIn account, delete specific data without closing your account, and what we remove or retain.

---

## Delete your entire account (all data)

### Option 1: Delete in the app (recommended)

1. Open **FitIn** and sign in.
2. Go to the **Profile** tab.
3. Scroll to **Support & legal**.
4. Tap **Delete account**.
5. Confirm **Delete account** in the prompt.

Your account and associated personal data are permanently deleted promptly after confirmation.

### Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email **dev@nfiny.com** with:

- **Subject:** `FitIn account deletion request`
- The email address linked to your FitIn account (Google or Apple sign-in)
- A brief note that you want your account and all associated data permanently deleted

We will verify ownership and process the request within **30 days**.

---

## Delete some data without deleting your account

You can remove specific data in the app while keeping your FitIn account:

| Data | Steps |
|------|-------|
| **Individual sets** | Open the workout in **Focus Mode** or **Workout report** → delete the set |
| **An entire workout** | Open **Workout report** for that session → delete the session |
| **Home gym / location pin** | **Profile** → home gym settings → **Clear home gym** |
| **Group membership** | **Groups** tab → select your name → **Leave group** |
| **Profile fields** | **Profile** → update or clear optional fields (e.g. body weight, home gym name) |

To request deletion of other specific data without closing your account, email **dev@nfiny.com** with the details above and describe what you want removed. We will process verified requests within **30 days**.

## What is deleted

### When you permanently delete your account

We remove:

- Account information (email, name, sign-in identifiers)
- Profile data (age, sex, height, goals, avatar, home gym)
- Workout sessions, sets, and body metrics
- Progress data (XP, levels, streaks, achievements)
- Gym check-ins and social feed events
- Group memberships
- Push notification tokens and authentication tokens
- Block and report records associated with your account

### When you delete specific data only

- **Sets / workouts:** The selected sets or sessions are permanently removed. Related progress (PRs, XP, achievements, feed events) may be recalculated or removed as applicable.
- **Home gym:** Stored gym name and coordinates are cleared from your profile.
- **Group membership:** You are removed from the group; your past activity may no longer appear in that group's feed or leaderboard.

---

## What may be kept

- **Deactivated accounts:** **Deactivate account** (Profile → Support & legal) hides your account but **retains all data** until you sign in again or permanently delete your account.
- **Shared groups:** If you delete your account but were in a group with other members, the group may continue without you.
- **Profile photos:** Disassociated from your account immediately on deletion; remaining storage copies may be purged within **30 days**.
- **Legal or security records:** Limited information may be retained where required by law or for fraud prevention, security, or dispute resolution.
- **Backups:** Deleted data may persist in encrypted backups for up to **30 days** before being overwritten.

---

## Retention period

| Action | Retention |
|--------|-----------|
| **Permanent account deletion** | Processed promptly; typically within **30 days** of confirmation or a verified email request |
| **Partial data deletion (in-app)** | Removed promptly when you delete the item in the app |
| **Partial data deletion (email request)** | Processed within **30 days** of a verified request |
| **Account deactivation** | Data retained until you reactivate or permanently delete your account |
