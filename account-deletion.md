# FitIn — Account & Data Deletion

**App:** FitIn (`com.nfiny.fitin`)  
**Developer:** nfiny-jae  
**Last updated:** 12 July 2026

You can request deletion of your FitIn account and associated personal data using either method below.

---

## Option 1: Delete in the app (recommended)

1. Open **FitIn** and sign in.
2. Go to the **Profile** tab.
3. Scroll to **Support & legal**.
4. Tap **Delete account**.
5. Confirm **Delete account** in the prompt.

Your account and personal data are permanently deleted promptly after confirmation.

---

## Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email us at **dev@nfiny.com** with:

- **Subject:** `FitIn account deletion request`
- The email address linked to your FitIn account (Google or Apple sign-in)
- A brief note that you want your account and data permanently deleted

We will verify ownership and process the request within **30 days**.

---

## What is deleted

When your account is permanently deleted, we remove:

- Account information (email, name, sign-in identifiers)
- Profile data (age, sex, height, goals, avatar, home gym)
- Workout sessions, sets, and body metrics
- Progress data (XP, levels, streaks, achievements)
- Gym check-ins and social feed events
- Group memberships
- Push notification tokens and authentication tokens
- Block and report records associated with your account

---

## What may be kept

- **Deactivated accounts:** If you choose **Deactivate account** instead of delete, your data is retained so you can sign in again to reactivate. Deactivation does not permanently delete data.
- **Shared groups:** If you were in a group with other members, the group may continue to exist without you. Your personal data is removed from that group.
- **Profile photos:** Photos stored in object storage are disassociated from your account immediately; remaining storage copies may be purged within **30 days**.
- **Legal or security records:** We may retain limited information where required by law or for fraud prevention, security, or dispute resolution.

---

## Retention period

- **Permanent deletion (in-app or email):** Processed promptly; typically within **30 days** of a verified request.
- **Deactivation:** Data is retained until you reactivate or permanently delete your account.
